the. moneyapp

FINTECH MOBILE APP

UI DESIGN UX WRITING

the. moneyapp was created as a UI Design Case Study. This is a finance mobile app, which focuses on creating saving goals, tracking spendings, and effective budgeting.

Problem Overview

Saving money or budgeting can be or seem difficult. Many people from different backgrounds or age groups have problem keeping track of their finances or recovering from excessive spending. Although many financial institutions provide similar services, they are not equipped with all the necessary features for a secure and thorough finance app.

Saving money can be difficult and seem hopeless, especially in our current economy. the. moneyapp helps users track their spending, provides plans for budgeting and saving, and rewards users when they achieve milestones.

User Flow

Solution

The solution is a mobile app. The application offers detailed transaction lists, a variation of charts, saving goals, a secure sign up process and the ability to track spending and budgeting.

The final product helps users create and fund personalized saving goals, track their spending and see their overall finances in one place. With the help of charts, icons, and encouraging texts, the user will be able to navigate their finances and achieve their goals.
