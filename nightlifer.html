NightLifer is a UI Case Study about a responsive web app, which focuses on location-based recommendations in the nightlife scene. The mission of the product is to help users navigate through a location’s nightlife, find information about businesses, and build a trusted community with reviews.

Problem Overview

After conducting a competitive analysis of hospitality apps, it was revealed that there’s no product on the market which solely focuses on a city’s nightlife and party scene. To find bars or clubs on any of the existing apps without having to face unnecessary results, like restaurants or 0-24 hour shops, and finding trustworthy and up-to-date information about businesses can be frustrating.

Navigating a city's nightlife can make having fun difficult. This is especially true if you're new to a city or just visiting. The NightLifer app helps users discover new places and find their way around a city's nightlife scene. It includes all the information needed to search for bars, clubs, and events. It even saves your favorite places and allows you to connect with your friends.

Solution

The solution is a responsive web app, that accessible across all devices. The application offers detailed business profiles, list and map search feature, live updates about how busy a place is, and a possibility to create Bar Tours with other users.

The final product helps users find and like places efficiently, access detailed information about a business, open the map to navigate easier, leave and read reviews. Additionally, users can create Bar Tours with other users to plan their night out easily.
